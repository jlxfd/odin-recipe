Buttermilk Waffles: Fluffy Pillows of Joy


Buttermilk Waffles

In the heart of Scandinavia, breakfast is not just a meal; it's a cherished ritual. And what better way to start your day than with a plate of Buttermilk Waffles? These golden, crispy-on-the-outside, tender-on-the-inside waffles are the epitome of comfort and indulgence.

Our recipe takes you on a journey through the art of waffle-making, revealing the secrets to achieving that perfect balance of sweet and tangy, with the unmistakable richness of buttermilk. Whether topped with fresh berries and a dollop of whipped cream or drizzled with maple syrup, these waffles are a morning delight that will make you feel like you've woken up in a cozy Nordic cabin. So, get your waffle iron ready and let's start your day the Nordic way!

Ingredients:

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Sour Cream
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Cardamom
  • Baking Powder

Steps:

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a medium bowl. Whisk the wet ingredients together in a separate large bowl. Pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and fold to combine, being careful to not to over mix.
  2. Make your waffles according the manufacturer's instructions on your waffle iron.
