Salmon Burger: A Nordic Twist on a Classic


Salmon Burger

When it comes to Nordic cuisine, salmon is king. And what better way to celebrate this majestic fish than with a Nordic-inspired Salmon Burger? Our recipe takes the beloved burger to new heights, infusing it with the bold flavors of the North.

Imagine a succulent salmon patty, perfectly seasoned with herbs and spices, topped with tangy Nordic pickles and a dollop of creamy dill sauce, all nestled within a soft, toasted bun. It's a taste explosion that marries the freshness of the salmon with the earthiness of Nordic herbs. Whether you're grilling in your backyard or sizzling up a storm in your kitchen, this Salmon Burger is a Nordic delight that will leave your taste buds singing the praises of the North.

Ingredients:

  • For the shrimp salad:
    • 5.30 oz (150g) shrimps, choped roughly
    • 1/2 red onion, sliced
    • 1/2 apple, cored and diced into small pieces
    • 1 Tablespoon capers
    • 3.5 oz (100g) mayonnaise
    • 1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
    • Salt & pepper
  • For the salmon:
    • 4 (preferably smoked) salmon fillets
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper
    • 4 sesame buger buns
    • 1/2 red onion, peele and sliced
    • 8 crispy lettuce leaves

Steps:

  1. For the shrimp salad: Mix shrimps, red onion, apple, capers, mayonnaise and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat the barbecue to high temperature. Brush the salmon fillets with olive oil and season. Briefly grill the salmon untill it gets a nice color, about 3-5 minutes on each side. Open the buns and slightly toast each surface on the grill.
  3. Place the lettuce leaves on the bottom of the buns. Garnish generously with the shrimp salad. Peel the skin from the salmon and place the salmon on top of the shrimp salad and put the lid on.
